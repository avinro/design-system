# Badge Partner

## Overview

Badge Partner is a pill-shaped label with the Dojo logo icon used on Venue cards to indicate that a venue is a Dojo partner. Its presence communicates that the user can interact with the venue (book or request). Its absence means the venue is not a partner and booking is not available at all.

Badge Partner is a single, fixed badge — there are no variants or types. It is display-only with no interactive states.

> **Presence = partner.** A venue either has the badge (Dojo partner, booking available) or it does not (not a partner, no booking).

## Anatomy

Badge Partner is composed of three parts:

| Part | Required | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Container** | Yes | Pill-shaped surface (border-radius: 9999px). Fixed cyan background. Padding: 8px horizontal, 4px vertical. |
| **Icon** | Yes | Dojo logo (`Customer-Icon/dojo-logo`), 16px. Decorative — must be `aria-hidden`. Color matches label. |
| **Label** | Yes | Fixed text: **"Dojo Partner"**. Manrope SemiBold, 12px, `whitespace-nowrap`. |

- The icon always precedes the label.
- Do not render Badge Partner without both icon and label.
- The label is fixed. Do not substitute custom text.

> **Note:** The Figma layer currently shows the label as "Dojo Parnet" — this is a typo. The correct label is **"Dojo Partner"**.

## Usage guidelines

- Show Badge Partner on a [Venue card](/components/ds-customer/content-display/venue-card/index) **only when the venue is a confirmed Dojo partner**.
- **Absence of the badge means the venue is not a partner** — the user cannot make a booking or request for that venue.
- Display **one Badge Partner per venue card** — the badge is not repeatable.
- Do not use Badge Partner as a general status or category label. For other status uses, use the main [Badge](/components/core/indicators-and-states/badge/index) component.
- Do not modify the label "Dojo Partner" or substitute custom text.

## Accessibility

- The Dojo logo icon is decorative. Apply `aria-hidden="true"` to it.
- The label "Dojo Partner" must be visible at all times — it carries the full meaning for screen readers.
- Do not rely on the icon or color alone to convey partner status — the label is always required.

## Do / Don't

**Do:**
- Show Badge Partner only on verified Dojo partner venues.
- Keep the label fixed as "Dojo Partner".
- Ensure the badge is visible and fully readable on the venue image.

**Don't:**
- Don't show Badge Partner on non-partner venues.
- Don't use Badge Partner to indicate booking type (bookable vs. request only) — that distinction is no longer represented at the badge level.
- Don't modify or replace the Dojo logo icon.
- Don't show multiple Badge Partner badges on the same card.
